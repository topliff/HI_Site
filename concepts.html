HaiQ (Human-AI Quotient)

As AI capabilities expand, the competitive edge lies in effectively partnering with it. HaiQ measures how well individuals and teams engage with AI while maintaining essential human capacities.

HaiQ tracks the quality of interaction between human imagination and AI augmentation across five dimensions. It is not a static score—it's a dynamic diagnostic embedded in our workshops through guided prompts, self-assessments, and team analytics.

Imaginative Agency

Using AI to expand, not replace, personal vision and creativity. This dimension measures how effectively individuals maintain their creative direction while leveraging AI tools to enhance their capabilities.

Reflective Depth

Maintaining self-awareness in AI-mediated workflows. This gauges one's ability to step back from technology, assess its influence, and maintain a critical perspective on AI-generated content and suggestions.

Collaborative Intelligence

Blending human empathy with AI pattern recognition. This measures how well teams integrate AI insights with uniquely human perspectives to create solutions neither could develop alone.

Purpose Alignment

Ensuring AI tools support meaningful, values-aligned work. This evaluates how well individuals and organizations keep AI systems oriented toward objectives that matter, rather than being led by what technology makes possible.

Adaptability

Responding to evolving AI contexts with coherence, not overload. This dimension tracks resilience and cognitive flexibility when navigating rapidly changing technological environments.

Why HaiQ Matters

HaiQ helps organizations evaluate not just what AI is doing but how well humans are growing alongside it. This balanced approach ensures technology augments rather than diminishes human potential and creativity.
