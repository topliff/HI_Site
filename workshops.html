AllStarTeams Workshop

Strengths-based team development for the modern workplace

AllStarTeams cultivates individual self-awareness, strengths-flow identification, and future growth vision while enhancing team collaboration through strengths-based fusion mapping. The program builds both individual insight and collective performance in a scalable, evidence-based framework.

Part 1: Individual Foundations

  • Develop deep self-awareness through our unique Star Self-Assessment
  • Discover your core strengths and natural flow states
  • Create powerful personal vision statements that guide growth
  • Connect present actions with long-term aspirations

Part 2: Team Practice

  • Develop strengths-flow fusion for optimized team performance
  • Build integrated team constellations based on complementary strengths
  • Map team dynamics visually for improved communication and collaboration
  • Create a shared team identity that honors individual contributions

Imaginal Agility Workshop

Imagination-centric team advancement for the AI era

Imaginal Agility develops imagination as a core capability, equipping individuals and teams for purposeful human-AI collaboration. This workshop focuses on developing the cognitive flexibility and creative capacity necessary for navigating rapidly changing environments.

Benefits

Traditional Assessment Renewal Offer (TARO)

Unlock greater value from your existing assessment investments

Key Strategic Advantages

  • Low-Resistance Entry Point: Enhances rather than replaces prior investments in training and certification
  • Strategic Partnerships: Complements assessment certification programs you already use
  • Custom alignment guides for each legacy assessment to maximize compatibility
  • Practical integration with team dynamics and role design within existing frameworks

Implementation

  • 2 guided 3-hour sessions designed to work with your existing assessment tools
